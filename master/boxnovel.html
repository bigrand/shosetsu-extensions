NOVEL BROKEN! Please Migrate!

Why Am I Seeing This?

A while ago, Novgo (Boxnovel) updated their website template (and their name: NovLove), which caused the existing extension to stop working. As part of the update, they also changed the URLs for novels and chapters. Unfortunately, it's not possible for us to automatically migrate your saved novels to their new site.

What Should I Do?

The good news is that Novgo (Boxnovel) has been updated to support the new website structure. While we can’t migrate your novels automatically, you can continue using the extension by manually searching for your novels again after updating the extension to version 4.2.0 or reinstalling it.

How to Migrate

  1. Update or reinstall the extension.
    • If you're seeing this message, it means you're already using the updated version (v4.2.0).
    • If you've updated Novgo (Boxnovel) but don't see the name change to NovLove (Boxnovel), don't worry, it has updated.
    • The name will appear correctly after reinstalling the extension.
  2. Search for your novel manually using the updated extension.
  3. Before migrating, make a note of which chapters you’ve already read to mark them as read later.
